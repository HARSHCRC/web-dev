Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 8 slices of bread
  • 8 slices of cheese
  • Butter, as needed

Instructions

  1. In a pan, cook the bacon over medium heat until crispy.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. On the unbuttered side of 4 slices of bread, place 2 slices of cheese and top with the cooked bacon and tomato slices.
  4. Cover with the remaining slices of bread, butter side up.
  5. Cook the sandwiches in a pan over medium heat until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  6. Serve and enjoy your Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato!
